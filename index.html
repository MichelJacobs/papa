What is PapaDAO?

Papa is a decentralized protocol based on the $HEC token – collateralized and backed by the Papa DAO. $HEC will be the reserve currency on Fantom. In order to maintain price stability Hector will use the Algorithmic Reserve Currency algorithm and will also be supported by other decentralized assets.
